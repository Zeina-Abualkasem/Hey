A Note

for Zeina

poetry was invented to describe you

I'm sorry I made you feel this way, I'm sorry that what I did can affect us, I'll do my best to do it right, make it better because I love you, and you know I do.

When I saw you, I was afraid to let you in because I built a lot of walls and guards around me. But when it happened, it felt like heaven, and I wish I could relive that feeling every day.

Honestly, sometimes I do just by getting lost in your eyes because that's it, actually, your eyes! It made me let my guards down. I stopped worrying about the damage you could cause or the damage I already carry.

I knew from day one that loving you would be like going to war and that I’d never return the same. But the truth is? I don't ever want to go back. I Love You.
