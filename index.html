an observation

for Zeina

in the deliberate space after missteps

To diminish someone’s presence requires a thoughtlessness I want no part in carrying forward. I built an unnecessary distance, and words alone cannot alter the architecture of how that felt.

But if you allow a moment, turn your attention here. I want to replace the noise with something steady. I recognize the specific clarity you bring into a room, the depth that exists entirely on your own terms.

I am sorry for the friction I created. I am sorry for causing an anger that you had every right to hold. I am choosing to move differently from this point onward.

“There is a profound intelligence in your stillness,
a quality I managed to misread
simply by failing to observe closely.”
